ENABLING 10 MILLION
PARENTS RAISE HAPPINESS

Creating MYLO - a full-stack
parenting platform

Parenthood, while being one of the most joyful life stages in our lives, has its challenges. And, for first-time parents, it can get even more stressful. The lack of knowledge, guidance & reliability of resources often leaves nuclear families in a state of constant struggle instead of celebrating the wonders of parenthood.
We discovered that the ‘desire for a trustworthy companion’ was the strongest need amongst new parents in this journey. They wanted a dependable partner that could guide them to make the right decisions based on real life experiences.

We positioned Mylo as a Life-Maximizer enabling
millions of parents to raise happy & healthy families.

The color-coding & design system was nuanced to the different journeys that 3 key cohorts took – TTC (Trying to conceive), pregnant & parents with up to 2 yrs child.

Personalized experiences were created through community, content & products clustering to ensure every user feels belonged, finds meaning & solutions to their unique challenges.

Mylo was transformed into a full-stack platform with product line inspired by real conversations of 10 million+ moms -the only platform to do so in india.
